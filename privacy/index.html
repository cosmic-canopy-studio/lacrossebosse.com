Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Lacrosse Bosse ("the app") is developed by Cosmic Canopy Studio. This policy explains what data the app collects.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no:

  • User accounts or sign-in
  • Analytics or tracking
  • Advertising
  • In-app purchases
  • Network connections to external servers

All play data is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services that collect data.

Future Features

When team features are introduced, the app may collect:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Team membership data (to coordinate play sharing)
  • Practice engagement metrics (to help coaches track progress)

This policy will be updated before any data collection begins.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. When age-related features are implemented, appropriate consent mechanisms will be added.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
